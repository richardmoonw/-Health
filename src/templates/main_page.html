What is +Health?

+Health pretends to gather all the patients’
medical information such as chronic diseases, medical
analysis, prescriptions, treatments, diagnoses and operations.



Explore Our Services


View Patient Medical History

Doctors can access the general information and see the patient medical history to give a more precise diagnosis.

Create Patient Diagnosis

Doctors can create and fill a new diagnosis for the patient, this will be saved in the patient medical history.

Create Patient Treatment

Doctors can prescribe a new diagnosis for the patient, this will be saved in the patient medical history.



Upload Medical Studies

Patients can upload their medical studies, these will be saved in the patient’s medical history.

View Medical Information

Patients and doctors can access to the patient general information to have an overall overview.

Validated Certificates

All the doctors using +Health have a certificate that was verified and validated by our team.
